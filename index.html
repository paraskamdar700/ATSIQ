From a Student Project to an AI-Powered Resume Scoring Revolution

Our platform is an intelligent, AI-powered resume evaluation system designed to help job seekers understand how well their resume aligns with modern Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). From a simple idea to a full-scale tech solution, our mission is to make job applications smarter, faster, and more effective.

Built using state-of-the-art technologies like React, Tailwind CSS, Java, Spring Boot, and MySQL, our platform scans resumes, analyzes them with a custom AI model, and generates a detailed score. Along with the score, users receive actionable feedback and tailored suggestions to improve their chances of landing interviews in today's competitive job market.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Our AI-driven ATS score checker bridges the gap between applicants and modern hiring systems by simulating how real-world Applicant Tracking Systems parse and rank resumes. It offers users a realistic, data-backed evaluation of their resume’s effectiveness, helping them understand how their application would perform in actual hiring pipelines. Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned professional, our system is designed to give meaningful insights that enhance resume quality and relevance.

By analyzing each resume for keyword usage, formatting issues, and content alignment, the platform delivers an instant ATS score along with smart, actionable suggestions for improvement. We aim to empower job seekers with tools that are typically available only to recruiters.
